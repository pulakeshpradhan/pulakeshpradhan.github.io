Google Earth Engine is a cloud-based platform that enables users to harness the power of satellite imagery for various applications. By providing a massive amount of geospatial data and the computational power to analyze it, Google Earth Engine supports applications ranging from deforestation monitoring to climate change modeling. It's a powerful tool for researchers, scientists, and developers aiming to make a positive impact on the planet.
