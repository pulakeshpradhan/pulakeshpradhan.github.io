Intersection of Climate Change, Agriculture & Rural Development

Vegetation

  • Vegetation Cover Mapping: Delineate areas of dense and sparse vegetation.
  • NDVI Analysis: Assess vegetation health using normalized difference indices.
  • Forest Health Monitoring: Track forest conditions and detect disease outbreaks.
  • Crop Identification: Classify crop types and monitor seasonal growth.
  • Invasive Species Detection: Detect and map non-native vegetation species.

Water

  • Surface Water Mapping: Identify lakes, rivers, and reservoirs.
  • Water Quality Assessment: Monitor clarity, pollutants, and algal blooms.
  • Reservoir Storage Estimation: Estimate water volume in storage facilities.
  • River Discharge Analysis: Analyze flow rates and water level variations.
  • Wetland Mapping: Identify and monitor the extent of wetland ecosystems.

Climate

  • Temperature Trends: Analyze long-term temperature patterns.
  • Precipitation Analysis: Monitor rainfall patterns and anomalies.
  • Extreme Weather Monitoring: Track storms, heatwaves, and cold snaps.
  • Climate Model Comparison: Evaluate projections from different climate models.
  • Seasonal Variability: Assess seasonal shifts in climate parameters.

Soil

  • Soil Moisture Analysis: Monitor moisture levels for agriculture and hydrology.
  • Soil Texture Mapping: Classify soils based on particle size and composition.
  • Erosion Monitoring: Identify areas at risk of soil loss.
  • Soil Organic Matter: Estimate organic content to assess fertility.
  • Land Degradation: Track changes in soil quality and productivity.

Topography

  • Digital Elevation Models (DEMs): Generate detailed elevation maps.
  • Slope & Aspect Analysis: Determine slope steepness and orientation.
  • Terrain Ruggedness: Quantify surface roughness and variability.
  • Contour Mapping: Create contour lines to represent elevation changes.
  • Watershed Delineation: Identify drainage basins and hydrological networks.

Ground Water

  • Aquifer Mapping: Identify and delineate groundwater reservoirs.
  • Groundwater Monitoring: Track water table fluctuations over time.
  • Recharge Estimation: Calculate natural replenishment rates of aquifers.
  • Contamination Risk: Assess areas vulnerable to groundwater pollution.
  • Well Location Analysis: Determine optimal locations for groundwater extraction.

Drought

  • Drought Severity Mapping: Identify regions under severe drought conditions.
  • Vegetation Stress Indicators: Monitor signs of water stress in plants.
  • Soil Moisture Deficit: Quantify lack of moisture in the soil.
  • Water Supply Shortfall: Assess potential shortages in water supply.
  • Drought Trend Analysis: Analyze historical drought patterns and trends.

Flood

  • Flood Inundation Mapping: Identify areas susceptible to flooding.
  • Flood Risk Zones: Define high-risk flood zones for planning.
  • River Floodplain Analysis: Assess floodplain dynamics and extents.
  • Urban Flood Detection: Detect flooding in urban landscapes via remote sensing.
  • Post-Flood Assessment: Evaluate damage and plan recovery measures.

Extreme Events

  • Heatwaves: Monitor extreme temperature spikes and durations.
  • Storm Surges: Track coastal flooding from intense storms.
  • Tornado Tracking: Identify and follow tornado paths.
  • Landslide Monitoring: Detect sudden landslides and avalanches.
  • Severe Thunderstorms: Monitor intense thunderstorm activity.

SAR Radar

  • Synthetic Aperture Radar Imaging: Acquire high-resolution images independent of weather.
  • Soil Moisture Retrieval: Derive soil moisture levels using radar signals.
  • Surface Deformation Analysis: Monitor land subsidence and uplift.
  • Vegetation Structure Mapping: Analyze canopy structure via radar backscatter.
  • Flood Monitoring: Detect flood extents using SAR even under cloud cover.

Degradation

  • Land Degradation Mapping: Identify regions experiencing environmental degradation.
  • Desertification Tracking: Monitor the progression of desertification.
  • Soil Erosion Rates: Measure rates of soil loss over time.
  • Deforestation Impacts: Assess how deforestation affects land quality.
  • Ecosystem Recovery: Track recovery progress in degraded areas.

LULC Change

  • Land Use & Land Cover Change: Analyze changes in landscape composition over time.
  • Urban Expansion: Monitor growth and sprawl of urban areas.
  • Agricultural Dynamics: Assess shifts in agricultural land and practices.
  • Forest Cover Change: Track deforestation and reforestation trends.
  • Habitat Fragmentation: Evaluate impacts of land conversion on ecosystems.
