Hi, I'm Pulakesh Pradhan

A Geographer with Research Interests in Climate Dynamics and Environmental Phenomena

Currently a Post-Doctoral Fellow at the Delhi School of Climate Change & Sustainability, I am dedicated to understanding complex climate systems. My research spans monsoon onset dynamics, climate variability, trend analysis, and climate modeling. With expertise in NCAR Command Language, R, GrADS, and Python, I explore the intricate relationships between climate variables and environmental changes.
